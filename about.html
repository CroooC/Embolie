About

  • Maya, a student at the end of her bachelor's degree in Fine Art at ECAL, has created a range of accessories. They include earrings with laser-cut shapes in several different shapes and sizes, in red, green and transparent. There are also 3D-printed white shapes in resin that can be attached with snap hooks wherever you like, and chain assemblies that can be worn as belts or necklaces. Accessories are, she believes, an easier and more accessible way of disseminating art.
